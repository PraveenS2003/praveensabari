Bluetooth based Homeautomation using Arduino UNO

Bluetooth-based home automation with voice recognition using Arduino is a system that enables control of various home appliances wirelessly. The setup involves an Arduino board equipped with Bluetooth connectivity and a voice recognition module. Users can give voice commands to the system, which is processed by the Arduino. The Arduino then communicates with the corresponding appliances via Bluetooth to execute the desired actions, such as turning on/off lights, controlling the temperature, or operating electronic devices. This technology provides a convenient and hands-free way to manage home automation tasks with ease.

Alcohol Sensing Engine Lock with GPS

The alcohol sensing engine lock with GPS is a device designed to prevent drunk driving incidents. It incorporates an alcohol detection system that analyzes the breath or blood alcohol level of the driver. If the level exceeds a pre-set limit, the engine is automatically immobilized. Additionally, the device is equipped with GPS technology to track the location of the vehicle, providing real-time information to authorities or designated individuals. This innovative solution aims to enhance road safety by effectively deterring intoxicated individuals from operating vehicles.
